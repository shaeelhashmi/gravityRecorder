# The Death of SaaS Privacy?

Every week, we hear about another cloud provider having a leak. When you record your internal meetings, your SaaS product demos, or your private code walkthroughs on a cloud-first recorder, you are taking a risk.

In 2026, the safest cloud for your data is your **own hard drive**.

## What is Local-First?

Local-first software combines the convenience of a web app with the security of a local application. **Gravity Recorder** is built on this principle.

### Your Video, Your Disk

## Zero Tracking, Maximum Speed

Because we don't track your every move, the app uses fewer system resources. This means more power for your screen capture and less lag during high-bitrate recordings.
